Mission Statement

TEKWW has the vision to spread technology throughout the world. We began in Ecuador, and will fund-raise to meet our goal of sending electronic devices in order to start computer labs. This project will expand; once we are past these initial ventures, future endeavours will be taken. Our objective of education through technology will enrich the learning place of others and introduce new and exciting technologies to developing nations.
