April 2013 - Lasso, Ecuador

TEKWW's first successful project

The TEKWW team travelled to Ecuador for their initial project over spring break from April 8 - 11. The objective for that trip was to visit the Juan Manuel Lasso School in Lasso, Cotopaxi Province, Ecuador. A computer lab of 10 Raspberry Pi devices was assembled, and an inauguration assembly held. Educational programs on the computers now supplement the curriculum of elementary-age students attending the school. Our vision of spreading education through technology was met.

August 2013 - Lasso, Ecuador

TEKWW's continued efforts in Ecuador

 

From August 5 - 11, TEKWW travelled to Quito, Ecuador in order to continue efforts to spread education through technology. The Juan Manuel Lasso School was revisited for the assembly of five additional computers, as well as a comprehensive training of administrators and teachers on the functionality of the Raspberry Pi. The curriculum of this elementary school was modified to fully include a new computing class.
