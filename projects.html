vibED

HackTX 2015 Top 10 Finalist

A productivity hack which monitors ambient environmental factors such as temperature, light, and noise. Users notified when their working environment is no longer conducive to their productivity.

Built using Intel Edison micro-controller, Arduino Grove sensors, IBM Bluemix, MongoDB, Firebase, and Swift.
